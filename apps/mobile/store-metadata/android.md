## Full Description

Pravado is your AI-powered visibility companion — the mobile extension of the Pravado platform that keeps you informed and in control of your brand's earned visibility, wherever you are.

Your Earned Visibility Index (EVI) tells you exactly how visible your brand is to AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google. The Pravado app surfaces your real-time EVI score, flags when it moves, and gives you the tools to act on it from your phone.

What you can do from the app:

MONITOR YOUR VISIBILITY
See your current EVI score, 30/60/90-day trend, and pillar breakdown across Visibility, Authority, and Momentum — all updated automatically.

ACT ON SAGE INTELLIGENCE
SAGE, Pravado's AI strategy engine, continuously monitors signals across PR, content, and SEO. When it identifies a high-impact opportunity, you get an action card you can approve or dismiss with a swipe.

TRACK AI CITATIONS
CiteMind monitors whether ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude are citing your brand in their responses. See new citations as they're detected.

MANAGE YOUR CONTENT
Review content in your pipeline, check CiteMind quality scores before publish, and approve drafts — all without opening a desktop browser.

TRACK YOUR PR
See the status of your active pitches, monitor journalist responses, and track which placements are driving EVI gains.

The Pravado mobile app is designed as a companion to the full Pravado platform at app.pravado.io. A Pravado subscription is required.
